# Recommendations for Q2

Based on Q1 performance, we recommend the following optimizations:

1. **Increase Retargeting Budget** — Cart abandoner segment delivered $8.45 CPA, significantly outperforming prospecting. Recommend shifting 15% of budget from prospecting to retargeting.

2. **Expand Premium Publisher List** — Allure and Byrdie delivered 1.1-1.2% CTR. Recommend securing premium placements or PMPs with these publishers.

3. **Reduce Health & Wellness Segment** — CPA of $215 is not viable. Recommend pausing or significantly reducing.

4. **Test Video Formats** — Pre-roll and outstream video inventory available at similar CPMs. Recommend 10% budget allocation to test.

5. **Improve Viewability** — 68% is slightly below target. Recommend implementing stricter viewability thresholds in DSP.
