# Statement of Work
## PixelPerfect Media — Lumina Skin Q1 2026 Programmatic Campaign

**Effective Date:** January 10, 2026

**Document Version:** 1.0 (Final)

---

## 1. Parties

**Client:** Lumina Skin, Inc. (via Zenith Media as Agency of Record)  
**Vendor:** PixelPerfect Media, LLC

---

## 2. Scope of Work

PixelPerfect Media will provide programmatic display advertising services for Lumina Skin's Q1 2026 campaign, including:

### 2.1 Media Planning & Buying
- Develop programmatic media plan aligned with client objectives
- Execute media buys across premium display inventory
- Manage real-time bidding and optimization

### 2.2 Audience Targeting
- Deploy first-party audience segments (provided by client)
- Build and activate lookalike audiences
- Implement site retargeting and cart abandonment campaigns

### 2.3 Creative Trafficking
- Traffic all client-provided creative assets
- Ensure proper ad serving and tracking implementation
- A/B test creative variants as directed

### 2.4 Reporting & Analytics
- Weekly performance reports (delivered Tuesdays by 12pm)
- Monthly optimization summaries
- End-of-campaign wrap report

### 2.5 Brand Safety & Verification
- Implement pre-bid brand safety filtering
- Monitor for invalid traffic
- Provide third-party verification (DoubleVerify)

## 3. Campaign Parameters

### 3.1 Budget

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| **Total Campaign Budget** | **$62,000** |
| Media Spend (net) | $52,500 |
| Platform/Tech Fee (15%) | $7,875 |
| Verification Fees | $1,625 |

**Budget Overages:** Any spend exceeding the contracted budget requires written approval from Zenith Media prior to execution. Verbal approvals are not sufficient.

### 3.2 Deliverables

| Metric | Guaranteed Minimum |
|--------|-------------------|
| Total Impressions | 16,000,000 |
| Viewability Rate | 70% |
| Brand Safety Score | 98% |
| Invalid Traffic (IVT) | <3% |

### 3.3 Targeting Requirements

**Geography:** United States only

**Audience Segments (priority order):**
1. Site Retargeting (past 30 days)
2. Cart Abandoners (past 14 days)
3. Lookalike — Past Purchasers
4. Beauty & Skincare Intenders
5. Luxury Shoppers

**Exclusions:**
- No political content adjacency
- No user-generated content sites
- No made-for-advertising (MFA) inventory
- Exclude competitor conquesting

### 3.4 Device & Format Mix

| Format | Allocation |
|--------|------------|
| Display (all sizes) | 100% |
| Video | 0% (out of scope) |

| Device | Allocation |
|--------|------------|
| Mobile | 55-65% |
| Desktop | 30-40% |
| Tablet | 5-10% |
