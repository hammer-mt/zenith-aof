### Key Creative Learnings

1. **Real > Polished:** UGC consistently outperforms brand-produced video by 40%+

2. **Transformation is king:** Before/after content performs 2x better than product-only

3. **First 3 seconds matter:** Transformation reveal and direct address hooks outperform

4. **Discount = danger:** Sale-focused creative tanks brand perception and long-term metrics

5. **Creator matters:** Same script performs 2-3x differently depending on creator authenticity

6. **Education works for high-intent:** Ingredient deep dives work in retargeting, not prospecting

7. **Dermatologist > Celebrity:** Dr. Patel content outperforms any celebrity quote we've tested
