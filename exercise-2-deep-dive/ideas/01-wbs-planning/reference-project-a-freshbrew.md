# WBS: FreshBrew Coffee — Summer Push (Completed)

**Client:** FreshBrew Coffee
**Budget:** $320K | **Duration:** 8 weeks (actual: 9 weeks) | **Channels:** Meta, Google Search, YouTube

---

## Summary

A straightforward 3-channel summer campaign for an existing client. The project followed the standard 4-phase workflow and came in close to plan, with two notable delays: brand assets arrived 3 days late from the client, and YouTube creative approval slipped 2 days. Vendor reconciliation took an extra week due to a Meta billing discrepancy, pushing the actual wrap to Week 9.

No new processes or channels were introduced. This project is a clean baseline for standard campaign execution with 3 or fewer channels.
