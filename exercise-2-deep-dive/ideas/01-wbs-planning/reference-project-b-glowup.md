# WBS: GlowUp Skincare — Holiday Campaign (Completed)

**Client:** GlowUp Skincare
**Budget:** $480K | **Duration:** 10 weeks (actual: 11 weeks) | **Channels:** Meta, TikTok, Google Search, Programmatic Display

---

## Summary

A 4-channel holiday campaign that introduced two new elements: audience research for a new client and programmatic display with PMP deals. The extra channel complexity added a week to media plan build (3 weeks vs. 2) and required a staggered launch — Meta and Google first, then TikTok and Programmatic one week later to manage QA.

Key mid-flight decision: budget was shifted from Display to TikTok during the optimization phase based on performance data. Vendor reconciliation ran into Week 11 as expected for a 4-vendor project. This project is the best reference for multi-channel campaigns with staggered launches.
