## Audience Insights

- 61% of engaged users were 18-34
- Top interest categories: Fitness, Health & Wellness, Food & Drink
- 72% of conversions came from creator Spark Ads (vs 28% from standard in-feed)
- Quebec engagement was 40% lower than other provinces — "limited French-language creator content"

## Key Wins

- "CTR 18% above target — creator content drives real engagement"
- "CPC 15% more efficient than target"
- "Over-delivered on all view metrics"
- "Creator content had 3x engagement vs brand-produced assets"

## Challenges

- "Spend came in 7% over budget due to strong performance in final week — auto-scaling was enabled"
- "French-language creator content gap limited Quebec performance"
- "GymBro_Tyler had highest reach but lowest efficiency — consider replacing for Q2"

## Recommendations

- "Double down on micro-creators (under 500K followers) — higher engagement, lower CPC"
- "Invest in French-language creator partnerships for Quebec"
- "Test TikTok Shop integration for direct purchase path"
- "Increase budget to $80K/month given strong ROAS trajectory"
