# Task: Summarize a Vendor Wrap Report Against the Original Brief

You are a media planner reviewing a vendor's campaign wrap report.

## Instructions

1. Read the vendor wrap report and the original campaign brief below
2. Produce a structured summary:
   - **Headline results** vs. the original objectives (did they hit targets?)
   - **What they're celebrating** that actually underperformed (vendor spin detection)
   - **Genuine wins** — metrics that truly exceeded expectations
   - **Red flags** — anything missing, vague, or inconsistent
   - **Key takeaways** — the 3-4 bullet points to include in the overall campaign performance deck

Keep it to one page. The planner needs to scan this in 2 minutes, not read 18 pages.

---

## Original Campaign Brief

**Vendor:** ReachMax Digital (programmatic display + native partner)  
**Client:** VitalFit Supplements  
**Campaign:** VitalFit Spring Awareness — Programmatic  
**Flight:** March 1 – March 31, 2026  
**Budget:** $25,000 net  
**Objectives:**
- 3,000,000 impressions (CPM target: $8.33)
- 0.12% CTR benchmark
- 80%+ viewability
- Brand Safety: No health misinformation, no competitor adjacency
- Frequency cap: 3x per user per week
- Geo: Canada only, with 40% in Ontario, 25% Quebec

**KPIs agreed in SOW:**
- Primary: Viewable impressions and brand lift (pre/post survey)
- Secondary: CTR, site visits from display
- Deliverables: Weekly pacing reports, mid-flight optimization report, final wrap report within 10 business days of campaign end
