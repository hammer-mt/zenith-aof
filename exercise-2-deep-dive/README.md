# Exercise 2: Deep-Dive — Make It Agent-Native

**Format:** 6 pre-submitted project ideas, ~5 minutes each  
**Total Time:** 30-40 minutes  
**Your Role:** Each participant presents their idea. You give them a rapid-fire "agent-native / agency of the future" reframe — what the AI handles, where the human stays in the loop, and what the first buildable version looks like.

---

## How It Works

1. Participant describes their idea (~1 min)
2. You reframe it through the agent-native lens (~4 min):
   - What's the **synthesis** the AI does? (the grunt work)
   - What's the **judgment** the human keeps? (the NCO Gap)
   - What's the **system improvement loop**? (New Taylorism)
   - What does **v1** look like? (something they could build this week)
3. Brief Q&A / reactions from the room
