# Greenfield Bank — Brand Guidelines (Abridged)

## Brand Essence

**Who We Are:**  
Greenfield Bank is the modern bank for people building their financial future. We combine the security of traditional banking with technology that actually makes sense.

**Brand Promise:**  
Banking that grows with you.

**Brand Position:**  
Unlike challenger banks that prioritize features or legacy banks that prioritize themselves, Greenfield prioritizes your confidence. We're not the flashiest. We're the one you'll actually trust with your money.

---

## Voice & Tone

### Voice Attributes

| Attribute | What It Means | What It Doesn't Mean |
|-----------|---------------|----------------------|
| **Confident** | We know what we're doing | Arrogant or dismissive |
| **Clear** | Plain language, no jargon | Dumbed down or patronizing |
| **Warm** | Human, approachable | Saccharine or fake |
| **Grounded** | Practical, realistic | Boring or corporate |

### Tone Calibration by Context

| Context | Tone Dial |
|---------|-----------|
| Brand awareness | Warm, aspirational |
| Product ads | Clear, confident, benefit-focused |
| Retargeting | Direct, helpful |
| Crisis/Service | Empathetic, solution-oriented |

### Examples

✅ **Do say:**
- "Put your tax refund to work with 4.25% APY."
- "No fees. No minimums. No catch."
- "Your savings, growing while you sleep."
- "Banking that makes sense. Finally."

❌ **Don't say:**
- "Adulting is hard!" (too trendy, not authentic to us)
- "Join the revolution!" (too aggressive)
- "What are you waiting for?" (too pushy)
- "We're not like other banks" (cliché)

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Greenfield Green (#2D5A4A), Cream (#F5F2EB)
- **Accent:** Warm Gold (#C9A85C), Soft Coral (#E8A598)
- **Neutral:** Charcoal (#333333), Warm Gray (#8C8C8C)

### Photography Style
- Natural light, warm tones
- Real moments, not stock photography feeling
- Diverse representation (age, ethnicity, life stage)
- Settings: homes, coffee shops, outdoors — not offices

### Typography
- Headlines: Editorial serif (approachable sophistication)
- Body: Clean sans-serif (readability)
- Avoid: All caps, excessive bold, decorative fonts

## Messaging Hierarchy

### Primary Messages (Rotate)
1. **Security:** "Your deposits are FDIC insured up to $250,000."
2. **Yield:** "Earn 4.25% APY on savings. Big banks pay 0.01%."
3. **Simplicity:** "Open an account in 5 minutes. No paperwork."
4. **No Fees:** "No monthly fees. No minimum balance. No hidden charges."

### Secondary Messages
- Mobile-first experience
- 24/7 customer support
- Instant transfers
- Joint accounts and family features

### Tertiary (Use Sparingly)
- Company history/founders
- Awards and recognition
- Partnership announcements

---

## Guardrails

### Never Do
- Make claims we can't substantiate (especially rate comparisons)
- Use fear-based messaging about competitors
- Promise outcomes we can't guarantee
- Use financial jargon without explanation
- Feature only young, single people (we serve all life stages)

### Always Do
- Include FDIC language on deposit-related claims
- Feature diverse representation
- Lead with benefit, not feature
- Include clear CTA
- Maintain warmth even in direct-response
