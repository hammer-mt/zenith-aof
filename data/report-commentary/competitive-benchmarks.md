## Key Insights

1. **Search is table stakes** — all competitors heavily invested, CPCs rising 8% QoQ
2. **TikTok is the battleground** — Current and Chime leading, others testing
3. **CTV is premium play** — SoFi and Ally using for brand building, hard to measure
4. **Retargeting efficiency declining** — audience fatigue across category, need creative refresh every 2-3 weeks
