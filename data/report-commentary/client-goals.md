# Greenfield Bank — Q1 2026 Campaign Goals

## Overview
Greenfield Bank is a digital-first challenger bank targeting millennials and Gen Z. Q1 focus is driving new savings account sign-ups ahead of tax refund season.

## Primary KPIs

### New Account Sign-ups
- **Monthly Target:** 4,000 new savings accounts
- **Weekly Run Rate:** 1,000/week
- **Acceptable CPA Range:** $25-45
- **Stretch CPA:** <$30

### Efficiency Targets by Channel
| Channel | Target CPA | Max CPA | Target ROAS |
|---------|------------|---------|-------------|
| Paid Search - Brand | $15 | $20 | 4.5x |
| Paid Search - Non-Brand | $35 | $50 | 2.0x |
| Paid Social - Retargeting | $18 | $25 | 4.0x |
| Paid Social - Prospecting | $30 | $40 | 2.5x |
| Display | $60 | $90 | 1.0x |
| Video/CTV | $80 | $120 | 0.8x |

### Secondary KPIs
- **Brand Search Volume:** +15% QoQ
- **Site Traffic:** 500K sessions/month
- **Email Capture Rate:** 8% of site visitors

## Budget Allocation (Weekly)
- Total Weekly Budget: $90,000
- Paid Search: 30% ($27,000)
- Paid Social: 40% ($36,000)
- Display: 15% ($13,500)
- Video/CTV: 15% ($13,500)

## Strategic Priorities
1. **Savings Account Push:** Tax refund messaging through mid-April
2. **Competitive Conquest:** Target customers of legacy banks (Chase, BofA, Wells)
3. **TikTok Scale:** Testing new channel, goal is prove/disprove viability by end of Q1
4. **CTV Incrementality:** Measuring lift from connected TV investment

## Notes from CMO (Sarah Chen)
- "I want to see us beating legacy bank CPAs by at least 20%"
- "TikTok needs to prove itself this quarter or we cut it"
- "Don't let CTV spend run ahead of proof — stay disciplined"
- "Brand search should be our most efficient channel, always"
