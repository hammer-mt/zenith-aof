# Lumina Skin — Q1-Q2 2026 Engagement Scope
## Zenith Media x Lumina Skin

### Engagement Overview

**Client:** Lumina Skin, Inc.
**Agency:** Zenith Media
**Period:** January 1 — June 30, 2026
**Monthly Retainer:** $45,000
**Media Budget (managed):** ~$350,000/month

### Scope of Work

#### Core Services (included in retainer)

1. **Media Strategy & Planning**
   - Quarterly media plans with channel allocation recommendations
   - Monthly budget pacing and reallocation
   - Competitive monitoring and response

2. **Campaign Management**
   - Meta Ads (prospecting, retargeting, brand awareness)
   - Google Ads (search, shopping, PMax, YouTube)
   - TikTok Ads (testing phase, with path to scale)

3. **Reporting & Analysis**
   - Weekly performance commentary (delivered Mondays by 12pm)
   - Monthly performance decks with insights
   - Quarterly business reviews

4. **Creative Support**
   - Creative briefing and feedback
   - Performance creative testing roadmap
   - Creator/influencer recommendations (TikTok)

#### Out of Scope (additional fees apply)

- Creative production (handled by client's in-house team + contractors)
- Landing page development
- Affiliate program management
- Programmatic display (currently handled by PixelPerfect)
- International markets

### Success Metrics (H1 2026)

1. Achieve blended ROAS of 3.3x+ across managed channels
2. Acquire 40,000+ new customers at <$18 CPA
3. Prove TikTok viability with <$25 CPA and 2-3 scalable creator partnerships
4. Maintain brand search impression share >85%
5. Support 25% YoY revenue growth target

### Known Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|------------|------------|

| Competitor aggression (GlowLab) | Medium | Increase brand search budget, monitor closely |
| TikTok fails to scale | Medium | Cap investment at 12% until proven |
| Creator content quality inconsistent | Medium | Establish clear briefs, approval process |
