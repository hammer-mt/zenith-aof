# Lumina Skin — Q1 2026 Media Goals
## Approved by Sarah Chen (CMO) on January 8, 2026

### Primary KPIs

| Metric | Q1 Target | Month 1 Actual | Month 2 Actual | Month 3 Target |
|--------|-----------|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| Total Media Spend | $1,020,000 | $320,000 | $345,000 | $355,000 |
| Blended ROAS | 3.3x | 3.1x | 3.2x | 3.4x |
| New Customer CPA | <$18.00 | $19.20 | $17.80 | $16.50 |
| Returning Customer CPA | <$8.00 | $7.20 | $6.90 | $7.50 |
| Total Conversions | 58,000 | 17,200 | 19,800 | 21,000 |

### Channel Mix Targets

| Channel | % of Spend | ROAS Target | Notes |
|---------|------------|-------------|-------|
| Meta | 55-65% | 3.5x | Primary acquisition channel |
| Google | 25-30% | 3.0x | Brand defense + non-brand growth |
| TikTok | 8-12% | 2.0x | Testing phase, efficiency secondary to learning |
| Affiliate | 3-5% | 4.0x | Managed separately |

### Strategic Priorities (ranked)

1. **Scale High LTV Acquisition** — Focus on customers with predicted 12-month LTV >$150. Willing to accept higher CPAs for this segment.

2. **Spring Sale Success** — March 15-31 promotion is our biggest of Q1. Target 40% revenue lift during promo period. Creative must feel premium, not discount-y.

3. **TikTok Proof of Concept** — Goal is to find 2-3 scalable creator partnerships and prove we can achieve <$25 CPA by end of Q1. Not about efficiency yet.

4. **Brand Search Defense** — Competitors (especially GlowLab) are bidding on our terms. Hold impression share >85% on core brand terms.

### What Success Looks Like (CMO's words)

"By end of Q1, I want to feel confident that we have a repeatable playbook for acquiring high-value customers at scale. I don't want to be over-dependent on Meta. And I want TikTok to feel like a real channel, not an experiment."

### Constraints

- Cannot use discount-focused messaging on non-sale periods (brand guideline)
- All creator content must be approved by brand team before going live
- No medical claims (FDA compliance)
- Retargeting frequency cap: 3x/day per user

### Reporting Cadence

- Weekly: Performance commentary + pacing (Mondays by 12pm)
- Monthly: Full deck with insights + recommendations (1st week of following month)
- Quarterly: Business review with CMO + CFO
