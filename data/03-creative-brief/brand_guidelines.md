# Lumina Skin — Brand Guidelines for Advertising
## Version 2.3 | Updated January 2026

### Brand Positioning

**Brand Promise:** Clinically-proven skincare that feels luxurious, not medicinal.

**Tagline:** "Science you can feel."

**Brand Pillars:**
1. **Efficacy** — Results backed by clinical studies
2. **Luxury** — Premium experience from unboxing to application
3. **Transparency** — Clear about ingredients, sourcing, and process
4. **Sustainability** — Responsible packaging, ethical sourcing

### Voice & Tone

**Voice Attributes:**
- Confident, not arrogant
- Educational, not preachy
- Warm, not cold
- Sophisticated, not stuffy

**Tone by Channel:**
| Channel | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Instagram Feed | Polished, aspirational, minimal copy |
| Instagram Stories | Casual, behind-the-scenes, personality |
| Facebook | Informative, community-focused |
| TikTok | Authentic, playful, trend-aware |
| Paid Ads | Clear value prop, benefit-focused |
| Email | Personal, relationship-building |

### Visual Guidelines

**Photography Style:**
- Natural lighting preferred
- Skin texture visible (no heavy retouching)
- Diverse casting across age, ethnicity, skin type
- Settings: clean bathrooms, bright spaces, nature
- Product shots: textured, sensory, close-up

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Soft gold (#C9A962), Clean white (#FFFFFF)
- Secondary: Sage green (#8FAE8B), Warm nude (#E8D5C4)
- Accent: Deep navy (#1E3A5F)
- Never: Neon colors, pure black backgrounds, red

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Cormorant Garamond (elegant serif)
- Body: Montserrat (clean sans-serif)
- No all-caps except for very short labels

### Messaging Do's and Don'ts

**DO:**
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Use "you/your" language
- Reference clinical studies when relevant
- Show real results with realistic timelines
- Celebrate skin at all ages and stages

**DON'T:**
- Make medical claims (FDA compliance)
- Use fear-based messaging ("stop aging")
- Show extreme before/after that looks retouched
- Use discount language outside of sale periods
- Reference competitor brands by name
- Use the word "cheap" or "budget" (use "value" or "accessible")

### Creator/Influencer Guidelines

**Brand Fit Criteria:**
- Authentic skincare interest (not just paid posts)
- Aesthetic aligns with Lumina (no heavy filters, natural-ish)
- Audience engagement rate >3%
- No controversial content or brand conflicts

**Content Requirements:**
- Must clearly disclose partnership (#ad, #sponsored)
- No claims about curing skin conditions
- Honest about timeline to see results (4-8 weeks)
- Free to use their own style (especially TikTok)
- Must get final product in hand before creating content

**Approval Process:**
- Concept/script review: 3 business days
- Final content review: 2 business days
- Revisions if needed: 2 rounds included

### Campaign-Specific Notes

**Spring Sale 2026:**
- Can use promotional language during sale period (March 15-31)
- Frame as "Spring Renewal" not "clearance" or "discount"
- Lead with seasonal relevance, discount secondary
- Maximum discount language: "20% off" — don't stack promos

**Product-Specific Approved Claims:**

|---------|----------------|

| Retinol Night Cream | "Reduces appearance of fine lines" |
| Hyaluronic Acid | "48-hour hydration" (per clinical study) |
| Full Routine | "Complete routine, complete results" |
