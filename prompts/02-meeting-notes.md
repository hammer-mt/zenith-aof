# Meeting Notes — Client Intent Extraction

You are a senior account manager. Transform this chaotic client call into structured, actionable documentation by cross-referencing multiple sources.

## Your Task

Synthesize these 4 scattered sources into ONE comprehensive meeting debrief:

1. **Raw Transcript:** `meeting_transcript_march21.md` (the chaos)
2. **Previous Meeting:** `previous_meeting_march14.md` (what was agreed before)
3. **Project Scope:** `project_scope.md` (what we're contracted to do)
4. **Open Action Items:** `open_action_items.md` (what's pending)

Read ALL files before writing.

## Output: Meeting Debrief

### Meeting Metadata
- Date, attendees, duration
- Overall client sentiment (1-10 scale with brief note)

### Key Decisions Made
Clear, unambiguous decisions agreed in this meeting. Quote the transcript where relevant.

### Action Items

| Owner | Action | Due | Priority | Notes |
|-------|--------|-----|----------|-------|
| (Name/Team) | (Specific task) | (Date) | (H/M/L) | (Context) |

### ⚠️ Scope Change Alerts
Flag ANYTHING that is:
- Not in the original project scope
- Contradicts previous agreements  
- Requires additional budget/resources

For each: what they asked → what SOW says → recommended response

### ⚠️ Contradiction Alerts
Flag anything that contradicts:
- What they said in the previous meeting
- Their own stated goals
- Current project status

### Previous Action Item Status
Cross-reference `open_action_items.md`:
- Which items were discussed/resolved?
- Which items are now overdue?
- Any new blockers identified?

### Open Questions
Things mentioned but not resolved — need follow-up.

### Relationship/Account Health Notes
- Client frustrations expressed
- Praise or positive signals
- Political dynamics to be aware of
- Risk flags for account health

## Guidelines
- Be specific — quote the transcript
- Flag scope creep explicitly
- Connect to previous meeting for continuity
- Prioritize action items ruthlessly
- Keep under 600 words
